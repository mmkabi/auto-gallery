# 90-Second Demo Script

## 0-10 seconds

Show the first-run screen, the Auto Gallery brand, Lyra — AI Vehicle Concierge, and **Explore. Understand. Decide.** Select **Start Guided Demo**.

Say: "The competition build uses a deterministic guided journey for a reliable presentation, with rule-based assistance available offline."

## 10-35 seconds

Let Lyra reveal the exterior in fixed Onyx Black, open the driver door, and enter the real cabin.

Say: "The optimized production asset preserves interactive doors, cabin, dashboard, seats, steering wheel, wheels, and material groups."

Pause briefly, then Resume to show operator control.

## 35-58 seconds

Let steering, wheel motion, rock-suspension mode, cargo inspection, and the conceptual powertrain view run.

Say: "The cargo fallback and conceptual powertrain labels are deliberate truthfulness decisions based on the source model."

## 58-75 seconds

Show the illustrative monthly payment and its labels **Demonstration Only** and **Not a Financing Offer**.

Show Lyra's local lead summary. Explain that no CRM record or customer data is transmitted by the guided journey.

## 75-90 seconds

Select **Restart Guided Demo** to prove clean repeat execution.

Say: "Auto Gallery connects product discovery, transparent commerce tools, and a repeatable 3D customer journey without depending on network latency."
