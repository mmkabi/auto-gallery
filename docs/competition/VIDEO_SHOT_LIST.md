# Video Shot List

## Recording Setup

- Primary capture: 1920x1080, browser zoom 100%, 30 or 60 fps.
- Backup capture: 1366x768, browser zoom 100%.
- Mobile insert: 390x844 portrait.
- Use a clean browser profile and one application tab.
- Hide bookmarks, downloads, notifications, developer tools, and personal accounts.
- Keep the cursor still during camera motion and move it deliberately between controls.
- Clear local demo records before recording.
- Confirm Onyx Black, exterior camera, doors closed, wheel motion off, terrain off, and Guided Demo label before the first take.

## Shot Sequence

1. First-run product statement over the vehicle.
2. Exterior hero without debug panels.
3. Paint swatches changing the body only.
4. Complete side-door open and close action.
5. Driver cabin and steering detail.
6. Lyra customer request and visible mode label.
7. Wheel focus and Rock terrain demonstration.
8. Cargo inspection disclosure.
9. Conceptual powertrain disclosure.
10. Illustrative payment calculation.
11. Consent and local demo record.
12. Clear demo data.
13. Brief judge diagnostics insert.
14. Exterior closing view with Auto Gallery wordmark.
15. Mobile layout insert.
16. Loading-state backup shot.

## Cursor and Motion

Use one click per action. Allow camera transitions to finish before moving to the next control. Avoid orbiting while panels animate. Do not record repeated failed clicks or use the debug panel in customer-facing hero frames.

## Audio

Use a calm voiceover and low-level licensed instrumental audio only if competition rules permit. The application intentionally contains no vehicle audio. Do not add unlicensed engine recordings or claim synthetic audio is real.

## Required Clean Screenshots

Store current images under `artifacts/competition/` with descriptive resolution suffixes. Exclude secrets, personal data, old branding, debug panels, and browser chrome where possible.
